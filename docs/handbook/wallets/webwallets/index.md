Stacy's Mom Bot & Rapthorus Bot

## Introduction
Stacys Mom (a Discord bot) and Rapthorus (a Telegram bot) are not traditional wallets.

They are bots that access core wallets and allow users on Discord and Telegram to own, receive, send, and gift virtual wallet addresses.

## Stacys Mom by Tri
Stacy's Mom is a Discord bot.

Once you're a member of the official RTM Discord chat, you'll automatically receive a Raptoreum Wallet address from Stacy.

With Stacy, you can receive, send, and gift RTM.

## Rapthorus by Germardies
Rapthorus is a Telegram bot.

Once you're a member of the official RTM Telegram chat, you'll automatically receive a Raptoreum wallet address from Rapthorus.

With Rapthorus, you can receive, send, and gift RTM.
