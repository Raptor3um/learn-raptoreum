Quick Guide 

1. Open the Raptoreum QT Wallet and wait until it has synchronized.
2. Click on Menu Create Asset.
3. Configure your asset as desired.
4. Click "Create Asset" below.
5. Read the notification regarding fees.
6. Enter your password.
7. Wait: Wait until the next RTM block is found. Only when the next RTM block is found will your asset be deposited into the blockchain.

    Note up to point 7:
    Once the block is found, your asset is created. It exists in the blockchain, but has a quantity of 0. To increase the quantity, you need to mint it:

8. Go to the Assets menu.
9. Select your asset.
10. Click on Mint in the right-hand menu.
11. Confirm the amount of fees.
12. Wait: Wait until the next RTM block is found. Only when the next RTM block is found will the mint be activated.
